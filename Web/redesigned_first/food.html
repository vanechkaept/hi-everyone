ABOUT US

In the center of Minsk on Karl Marx street (former Piedmont), near the Officers ' house, one of the masterpieces of the Minsk constructivism, 2011 restaurant "KOhms". Kuhmistr-in German - "master, or master of the kitchen")-is the same as today's chef. So called in the old Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth, including the Grand Duchy of Lithuania, chief of cooks, the commander of the Royal, princely and Ducal kitchens, who were invited first from Italy and France, but gradually replaced them with local students.


We also made sure that the music and video atmosphere of our restaurant matches our menu, So that you not only have a delicious meal, but also leave a holistic impression of the history and culture of our country, which for centuries was at the junction of two civilizations, and was a hospitable home for many religions and cultures.
